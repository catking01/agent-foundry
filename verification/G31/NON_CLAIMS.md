# G31 Non-Claims

G31 does not claim:

- a real-world optimal organization structure
- a real-world best AI organization policy
- validation of multi-agent organization theory
- real AI supervisor, lead, or worker execution
- Runtime Lab validation
- Ollama or live LLM participation in the policy search
- production governance readiness
- exhaustive organization-design search
- that deterministic simulator results generalize to real organizations

Correct claim:

Agent Foundry includes a read-only Research UI for G30 deterministic organization policy search summaries.
