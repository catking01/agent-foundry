# G31 Policy Search Screenflow

## Entry

Open the app and navigate to the Research tab.

## Visible Sections

1. Existing G27/G28 deterministic study panels remain visible.
2. G31 appears after the G27/G28 research sections as `Deterministic policy search results`.
3. The G31 metrics bar shows read-only mode, 288 G30 runs, 12 policies, 8 seeds, 5 objectives, and sealed evidence.
4. The overview card shows the matrix shape and source commit.
5. The objective rankings table shows the top 3 policies for speed, quality, risk reduction, coordination efficiency, and balanced score.
6. The Pareto frontier section shows frontier policies, objective scores, dominated policies, and dominance-pair count.
7. The order complexity breakdown shows best policy and aggregate metrics for simple, medium, and complex order classes.
8. The policy config details table shows the 12 curated policy configurations.
9. The scoring policy section shows deterministic formulas and interpretations.
10. Risk semantics and non-claims panels remain visible.

## Read-Only Boundary

The screenflow has no mutation controls. It does not start runs, apply policies, dispatch actions, or mutate game state.
